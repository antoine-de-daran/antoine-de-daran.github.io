FAQ

A few things people usually ask.

How can I get an update when a new post comes out?

Follow me on LinkedIn where I share every new post. If you prefer a feed reader, grab the RSS feed and you'll never miss one.

What if I see something incorrect in a post?

Please let me know! Send me a quick message on LinkedIn and I'll get it fixed. I really appreciate the help.

How can I support your work?

You can sponsor me on GitHub Sponsors or buy me a coffee on Ko-fi. Sharing a post with someone who might find it useful helps just as much.
